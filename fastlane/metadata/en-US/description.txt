Crew Link keeps your convoy together – in real time.

LIVE MAP
See all convoy members on a shared map. GPS positions update in under 2 seconds so you always know who is where.

DISTANCE WARNING
Falling behind? Crew Link automatically alerts all members the moment the gap exceeds 500 m – before the group splits up.

PUSH-TO-TALK WALKIE-TALKIE
Hold the PTT button and talk to your group instantly. Opus codec over WebRTC keeps latency under 300 ms regardless of connection quality.

CARPLAY INTEGRATION
Full Crew Link control on the CarPlay screen: convoy status and PTT button, no need to touch your phone while riding.

VEHICLE PROFILES
Enter your motorcycle's make, model, year, and modifications. Everyone in the convoy sees what you ride.

CONVOY IN 3 TAPS
Create a convoy, share the invite link, done. Joining is just as fast.

Privacy: Location data is used exclusively for live coordination within active convoys. It is never stored permanently or shared with third parties.
